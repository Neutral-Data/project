What is NeutralData?


NeutralData is an application born from a TFG designed to detect sensitive terms in data set files.

Some of the actions you can take are the following:


  • Upload a file from your PC in .csv or .xlsx format and detect sensitive terms and profanity.
  • Choose from one of your public repositories on GitHub, a file in .csv or .xlsx format and perform the detection of sensitive terms and profanity.
  • Once the detection is finished, you will be able to download the clean file in the same input format.
